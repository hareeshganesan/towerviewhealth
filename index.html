Our Story
TowerView Health was created after our college roommate and co-founder, Nick, was diagnosed with leukemia. Meidication is meant to help improve lives, not complicate them. It's one small step to bring peace of mind to Nick, his family, and millions like him.
Who is involved?
Taking meds should be easy. We bring nurses, doctors, and pharmacists together to ensure your patients never miss a dose of their medication. You and your patients should not be worrying about whether they've taken their meds or what they're taking, so we're here to make it easier for everyone.
